About EGDA

EGDA works to strengthen and grow Estonia's game development ecosystem.

We connect developers, studios, educators, cultural institutions, and industry partners through events, collaboration, education, and advocacy. We support the next generation of game developers, build connections with partners in Estonia and abroad, and promote games as an important part of contemporary culture and the creative industries. Our goal is to build a strong, connected, and sustainable game development community in Estonia.

Want to become a member?

Whether you're a developer, studio, student, publisher, educator, hobbyist, or simply passionate about games, you're welcome to become part of the community. Together, we're building a stronger, more connected, and internationally recognised Estonian games industry.
