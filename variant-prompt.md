# Variant Prompt For EGDA Website

Create a polished website mockup for EGDA, the Estonian Game Developers Association, a new non-profit organisation representing and supporting Estonia's video game industry.

Context:
EGDA is replacing a damaged public trust environment around Estonian game development after the previous organisation, GameDev Estonia, shut down following financial and tax-related controversy. Do not mention accusations or drama on the public-facing site. The website should feel like a clean reset: credible, transparent, community-oriented, modern and serious, while still clearly belonging to game development culture.

Main goal:
Build a website that helps game developers, students, studios, educators, researchers, cultural institutions, public sector partners, publishers, hobbyists and non-profits understand EGDA's mission and apply for membership. The page should improve the image and reputation of game development in Estonia, build community, and make membership feel open and worthwhile.

Required content:
- Brand/name: EGDA, Estonian Game Developers Association.
- Domain signal: egda.ee.
- About EGDA: a non-profit organisation representing and supporting Estonia's video game industry.
- Mission: strengthen the growth of game development in Estonia by connecting studios, developers, educators, researchers, cultural institutions and public sector partners.
- Positioning: games sit at the intersection of technology, creativity, storytelling, art and innovation, and are an important part of contemporary digital culture and Estonia's creative economy.
- Focus areas: visibility for the Estonian games industry, talent development, collaboration between industry and education, international partnerships, advocacy for games as a creative economy sector.
- Membership: applications are open and everyone is welcome to join, including game developers, students, publishers, hobbyists, studios and non-profits.
- Membership benefits: representation in Estonia's game development community, access to shared industry contacts/platforms/publishers with consent, participation in general meetings and voting, updates about EGDA activities/initiatives/budget use, ability to shape the future of Estonian game development.
- Contact: info.egda@gmail.com.
- Discord: https://discord.gg/mTkj64Aj4.
- Membership form: https://forms.gle/4EGerrgaZxP6MWcAA.
- Include an EGDA News section and a Game News section. If there is no real news content, show them as structured areas ready for updates rather than inventing fake article dates or fake headlines.

Visual direction:
Use a dark game UI aesthetic, but keep it refined and association-appropriate. Think retro-futuristic strategy game dashboard rather than childish arcade. The site should use deep navy-black or near-black surfaces, crisp cyan and magenta neon accents, thin HUD-style borders, subtle scanline or CRT texture, modular panels, progress-meter-like dividers, and sharp square geometry. Avoid soft generic SaaS cards, beige/cream palettes, huge rounded corners, gradient blob backgrounds, fake stock photos, and playful mascot imagery.

Suggested palette:
- Background: #0A0014 or very dark navy-black.
- Panels: #0C1024 or translucent black-blue.
- Primary accent: cyan #00FFFF.
- Secondary accent: magenta #FF006E.
- Limited highlight: amber #FFB000.
- Text: near-white #F3FBFF.
- Muted text: blue-grey #A5B7C7.

Typography:
Use a game-native but readable pairing, such as Orbitron for display headings and Space Mono for body/UI copy. Headings can be uppercase and angular. Body copy must remain legible and professional. Avoid goofy pixel fonts for long text.

Layout:
Make the first viewport the real website experience, not a marketing placeholder. Include a sticky top navigation with Mission, Membership, News and Contact. The hero should clearly show "Estonian Game Developers Association" as the primary signal, with a short mission paragraph and two calls to action: "Apply for membership" and "Join Discord." Use a right-side or secondary HUD panel listing the association's core objectives: visibility, community, collaboration.

Sections:
1. Hero: EGDA name, mission, membership CTA, Discord CTA, dark game UI visual background.
2. Mission: explain the ecosystem-building role with four focus blocks: Industry visibility, Talent development, Public advocacy, Partnerships.
3. Membership: explain that applications are open and list the real membership benefits.
4. News: split into EGDA News and Game News areas without fake dated posts.
5. Contact: email, Discord and membership form.

Tone:
Clear, confident, transparent and community-first. Do not overuse hype language. Avoid vague AI-style lines like "unlock the future" or "where innovation meets creativity." The site should feel like a trustworthy organisation that understands game culture and is ready to rebuild confidence.

Interaction ideas:
Add subtle hover states on navigation and CTA buttons, light panel glow on active/hover states, and small HUD-style meters or dividers. Keep motion restrained and accessible.

Responsive behavior:
Desktop should feel like a wide game dashboard with a strong hero and modular panels. Mobile should collapse into a clean vertical interface with full-width buttons, readable body text and no overlapping decorative elements.
