Estonian Game Developers Association

EGDA is a new non-profit organisation representing and supporting Estonia's video game industry.

Apply for membership Join Discord
What EGDA does Estonia

Connect

Bring together developers, studios, students, educators and partners.

Support

Help the local game development community share contacts, knowledge and opportunities.

Represent

Improve visibility for games as part of Estonia's creative economy.

Strengthen game development in Estonia

EGDA exists to connect people, support talent development, improve industry visibility and encourage collaboration between games, education, culture and the public sector.

FocusVisibility

Industry visibility

More recognition for Estonian studios, developers and game projects.

FocusTalent

Talent development

Better links between students, educators and people already working in games.

FocusCulture

Games as culture

Support for games as part of contemporary digital culture and creative economy work.

FocusPartners

Partnerships

Useful connections with local and international partners, platforms and publishers.

Membership applications are open

Everyone is welcome: developers, students, publishers, hobbyists, studios, non-profits and people who want to contribute to Estonian game development.
