# Balanced Blue Accents Design

## Goal

Reduce monochrome stretches across the EGDA landing page while preserving its dark, cream, and electric-blue brutalist identity.

## Direction

Use the existing `--blue: #1C00FF` as a deliberate hierarchy and interaction color rather than introducing a broader palette.

## Changes

- Make section badges and small italic section titles blue.
- Add selective blue emphasis to important headline words and list punctuation.
- Add restrained blue rules or corner details to major content panels.
- Change general interactive hover states to blue backgrounds with cream foregrounds.
- Give the membership and application area a stronger blue anchor while keeping its body-copy panels dark and readable.

## Constraints

- Keep the animated hero background and board portrait treatment unchanged because they already carry strong blue presence.
- Do not turn whole content sections blue or reduce body-text contrast.
- Preserve the existing typography, grid, spacing, content, and responsive structure.
- Prefer reusable CSS classes and variables over scattered inline color values.

## Verification

- Review the page at desktop and mobile widths.
- Inspect the hero, goals, membership, updates, board, and footer areas.
- Confirm blue accents are distributed through the full page without dominating any one section.
- Confirm hover states remain legible and no layout or interaction regressions appear.
