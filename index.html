Community Mission

EGDA brings together people working in and around games in Estonia. We are building a useful association to support the local game development community.

Developers Students Studios Educators Publishers Non-profits

Who Can Join

Everyone is welcome: developers, students, publishers, hobbyists, studios, non-profits and people who want to contribute to Estonian game development.

Membership applications are open.

Membership Benefits

  • Get represented in Estonia's game development community
  • Access shared industry contacts, platforms and publishers, with consent
  • Take part in general meetings and voting
  • Stay updated on EGDA activities, initiatives and budget use
  • Help shape how the local game development community grows
